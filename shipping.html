RETURNS & SHIPPING

Returns Policy

THANK YOU FOR YOUR PURCHASE!
In case you are not completely satisfied with the product for any reason, you may return it to us for a full refund or an exchange.

PRIOR TO MAKING ANY RETURN/EXCHANGE YOU MUST CONTACT US VIA EMAIL WITH YOUR REASON FOR RETURNING THE PRODUCT, FULL NAME AND ORDER REFERENCE.

  • All returns must be postmarked within 14 days of the purchase date. All returned items must be in NEW and UNUSED condition, with all original tags and labels attached. In case the skateboard shows any sign of usage such as scratched board, dirty grip, etc, - you will not be entitled to a refund.
  • To return an item place the item securely in its original packaging with your order reference and post it to the address we have sent you via email.
  • After receiving your returned item and inspecting the condition of the longboard, we will handle your request. If your request is accepted, please allow at least 5 working days from the receipt of your item for the refund to be processed.
